Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026.

The short version: GameReview does not sell your data, does not run third-party ad trackers, and stores your review data locally in your own browser. We keep things as simple and private as possible.

What Data We Collect

When you submit a review, the name you provide and your review text are stored in your browser's local storage. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server. We do not require account registration or an email address to use the site.

Local Storage

GameReview uses browser local storage to save your submitted reviews so they persist between visits. This data is stored entirely on your own device. You can clear it at any time by clearing your browser's site data for this domain. We have no access to this data.

Cookies

We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or analytics cookies. The site may use a small session cookie for basic functionality, but no personally identifiable information is stored in it.

Third Parties

GameReview does not embed third-party advertising, social media tracking pixels, or analytics platforms. No data about your browsing activity on this site is shared with outside companies.

Changes to This Policy

If we make significant changes to this privacy policy, we will update the date at the top of this page. We recommend checking back occasionally. Questions about privacy can be directed to us via the Contact page.
