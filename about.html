Our Mission

GameReview was founded in 2026 with a single goal: to give players honest, thoughtful coverage of the games that matter. We believe in treating games as the art form they are — worthy of serious criticism, deep analysis, and genuine passion.

What We Do

Our team curates and aggregates reviews from both professional critics and the gaming community. Every score on GameReview reflects real opinions from real people who have spent serious time with each title. We do not accept payment for reviews or allow advertisers to influence our editorial scores.

Community Reviews

In 2026 we opened our platform to community submissions. Any registered user can write a review and have it published alongside our editorial coverage. Community scores are calculated separately and transparently so you always know who is behind a rating.

The Team

GameReview is built by a small team of developers and writers who genuinely love games. We are always looking for passionate contributors — if you write about games and care about doing it honestly, reach out through our Contact page.
